Account Deletion Request Procedure

Users who have created an account through the Application may request deletion of their account and associated personal data by using the dedicated public account deletion page or by submitting a request via email.

In order to enable us to process a deletion request securely and accurately, the request should include sufficient information to identify and verify the account holder, including, where applicable:

  • the full name of the account holder;
  • the name of the Application, namely Si Paten Bandung;
  • the username, registered email address, phone number, or other account identifier used in connection with the Application;
  • a clear statement requesting the permanent deletion of the account and associated personal data; and
  • contact information that may be used to verify the identity of the requester and confirm completion of the process.

Upon receipt of a valid request, we may take reasonable measures to verify the identity of the requester before processing deletion. Following successful verification, the account will be deleted or permanently deactivated and associated personal data will be removed within a reasonable period and, in general, no later than 14 business days, except where retention is required or permitted by applicable law or is necessary for fraud prevention, dispute resolution, enforcement of legal rights, or security purposes.

Deletion of an account may result in permanent loss of access to the Application, user history, preferences, submitted content, and other account-linked data. Limited categories of data, such as security logs and records required to be retained by law, may be preserved for a limited period following deletion. Aggregated or anonymized information that no longer identifies an individual user may also be retained.
